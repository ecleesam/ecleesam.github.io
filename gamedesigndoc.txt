Game Title: Asteroids

Game archetype: Shooter/Dodger Hybrid

Goal: Destroy asteroids by shooting at it, avoid collisions and collect points.

Inspired by classic arcade game with the same name.

Controls: arrow keys or WASD to move, spacebar or K to shoot. 

Pause and Restart implementation should be included.

Player starts with 3 lives, Large asteroids give 50 points, Small asteroids give 20 points.

Collision with Asteroids takes 1 life.
